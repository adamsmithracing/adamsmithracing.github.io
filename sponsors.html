Sponsors

SPONSOR REPRESENTATION

Adam offers a variety of sponsorship packages and is open to discussing all opportunities.
Below are listed a variety of ways Adam can promote and benefit your brand.

BRAND AWARENESS

  • Participation event of your choice.
  • Logo on autograph cards, t-shirts, hoodies and all other merchandise.
  • Mention of brand in all interviews including video, radio, print and track interviews.
  • Brand logo seen on car at race track and races broadcast online.
  • Brand logo seen on car on social media posts and articles with photos of the car.

SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY

  • Posts of brand logo/product on social media outlets.
    • Instagram & Facebook follower reach.
    • Includes "Sponsor Sunday' shout out of company and website link.
  • Brand logo and company website link on adamsmithracing.com.

PUBLIC RELATIONS & PROMOTION

  • Appearances as a brand representative at locations.
  • Attending events and gatherings as requested by sponsor.

RESPECTABILITY & SPORTSMANSHIP

  • Adam reflects well upon every brand & is an inspiration to kids following their dreams.
  • Opportunity to be a part of Adam’s journey.
