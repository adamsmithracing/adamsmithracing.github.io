About Adam

Hometown: Houston, Texas

Born in Texas Adamâ€™s pride of being a Texan is huge!! Being a military brat and being in the military himself, Adam moved all over and finally made his way back to his beloved state in 2021.

Adam cut his teeth in racing by way of motorcycle road racing and found himself on the podium 3 times in 4 seasons. After a couple injuries that landed him in the hospital he then decided to move to 4 wheels.

He first attended the Skip Barber Racing School as an Intermediate and the returned to the Advanced School and ended up taking 2nd in his class. From there, Adam went on to Formula Mazda and enjoyed some success by getting his first ever pole on qualifying as well as two top five finishes.

Adam has always had a love for oval tracks and stock cars. After coming out to one of the races at Houston Motorsports Park, he talked with MCM Racing Development about being a potential driver. Things worked out and Adam will be driving the Allison Legacy stock car! He is immensely proud to be on such a successful team and has signed a 1.5 year contract with MCM Racing Development.

Adam is both excited and ready to bring MCM Racing Development more success!
